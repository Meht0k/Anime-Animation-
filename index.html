漢字(Kanji)

Kanji are logographic characters in the Japanese writing system, originating from Chinese. They convey meaning, pronunciation, and are vital for written Japanese. They often consist of radicals, with specific stroke orders. Learning kanji is essential in mastering the language.

佐藤健太 (Itachi Uchiha)

Itachi Uchiha is a complex character in the "Naruto" anime series. He's a prodigious ninja with tragic motives. Itachi's actions, including the massacre of his clan, are driven by a hidden agenda aimed at protecting the village from a greater threat.

孫悟空 (Goku)

Goku, a fictional character in the "Dragon Ball" series, is an iconic and powerful Saiyan warrior. Known for his boundless energy, superhuman abilities, and a quest for constant improvement, he's a beloved symbol of determination and heroism in anime and manga.
